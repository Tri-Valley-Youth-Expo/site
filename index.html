Overview

At the Tri-Valley Youth Expo, the region’s largest exposition of youth art, music, technology and culture, we aim to capture the true essence of what it means to be part of such an amazing, innovative and diverse community. We are believers that the talents and creativity of students today will shape our future; and we’re happy to offer a platform for the youth community in the Tri-Valley to start changing the world. To us there is little more powerful and meaningful than a united, liberated community — where passion is encouraged, progress happens!
